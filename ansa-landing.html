What ANSA does

ANSA is a structured assessment that captures an adult’s needs and strengths across life domains. Providers complete it in the assessment system to support service planning and to share a consistent picture of the person across DHS.

Who it helps

  • Adult-serving DHS providers completing or updating an ANSA.
  • CYF providers who use the ANSA/CANS overview and reports.
  • Supervisors reviewing assessment history and reports.

How to get access

Sign in through Okta with your county credentials and open the assessment tile. New to ANSA? Request access through the ISAR form first, then look for the tile in Okta.

Heads up: some current job aids still open with the older KIDS login screen. Access is now through Okta — that mismatch is exactly why these guides are flagged “needs review.”
